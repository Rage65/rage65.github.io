Personal Assistant

How to set up your own voice assistant powered by AI

This article will walk you through setting up home assistant, ollama, and wyoming satilite in order to make your own smart assistant

Note: do this first step on a pc with a decent gpu and a fast ssd for best preformance

To start you need to install ollama. To install it go to Ollama.com then click on download and follow the steps for your os.

once its installed open up your terminal and type

ollama pull llama3.2:latest
