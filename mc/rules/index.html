Minecraft Server Rules

Here you will find all the rules you need to follow while playing on the server.

1. Dont be offensive to other players.

2. No griefing or destroying other players' builds.

3. Keep chat mostly appropriate.

4. No cheating or exploiting bugs.

5. Respect the server staff and their decisions.

6. Have fun and make it fun for others!

Failure to comply could result in a warning, a temporary ban, or a permanent ban.
